Seafood Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
500g Pasta
500g Mussels
500g Clams
250g Cuttlefish / Squid
8 Scallops
20cl White wine

Preparation

  1. Cook the mussels and clams in white wine over high heat until they open.
  2. Cut the cuttlefish/squid into strips and sauté them in hot oil. Season with salt and pepper, and add chopped garlic,
    , basil, and parsley. Pour in the strained cooking juice, then let it simmer for 30 minutes over low heat.
  3. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water, then drain them.
  4. Sear the scallops for 2 minutes in butter.
  5. Transfer the pasta into a sauté pan with the seafood, heat it all together, stir, and serve it hot.

Additional Notes

  • Make sure to clean the seafood properly before cooking.
  • Adjust seasoning according to taste preferences.
  • This recipe serves approximately 4 people.

White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
400g Pasta
2 cups Milk
3 tbsp Butter
2 tbsp All-purpose flour
1/2 cup Grated cheese
1/4 tsp Black pepper
1/4 tsp Dried oregano

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add flour and cook for a minute, stirring continuously.
  3. Slowly add milk while stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Add grated cheese, black pepper, and dried oregano to the sauce. Stir until cheese melts and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the saucepan and mix well until the pasta is coated with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with fresh herbs or additional cheese if desired.

Additional Notes

  • You can add vegetables like broccoli or mushrooms for extra flavor.
  • Adjust the consistency of the sauce by adding more milk if needed.
  • This recipe is suitable for vegetarians.

Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 1/4 cups (280g) All-purpose flour
1 teaspoon Sugar
1 teaspoon Salt
1 tablespoon Olive oil
1 cup Warm water (110°F-115°F / 43°C-46°C)
1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) Active dry yeast
1/2 cup Pizza sauce
2 cups Shredded mozzarella cheese
As needed Assorted toppings: sliced pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, sliced mushrooms, etc.

Preparation

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a soft dough forms.

Additional Notes

  • You can customize toppings based on your preference, such as adding olives, onions, or different types of cheese.
  • For a crispier crust, pre-bake the crust for a few minutes before adding sauce and toppings.
  • This recipe makes one large pizza or several smaller ones depending on the size you prefer.

Perfect Rice Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cup Long-grain white rice
1 3/4 cups Water or chicken/vegetable broth
1 tablespoon Butter or olive oil (optional)
To taste Salt

Preparation

  1. Rinse the rice in a fine mesh strainer until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the rinsed rice, water (or broth), butter or olive oil, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid, and let it simmer for 18-20 minutes.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the rice sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes to steam.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.

Additional Notes

  • You can add other seasonings or herbs to the rice for extra flavor, such as garlic powder, onion powder, or chopped herbs.
  • For extra fragrance, you can toast the rice in a bit of oil or butter before adding the liquid.
  • This recipe yields perfectly cooked, fluffy rice.

Brownie Cake Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cup (225g) Butter
2 cups (400g) Granulated sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
4 large Eggs
3/4 cup (90g) Cocoa powder
1 cup (125g) All-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Baking powder
1/2 cup (60g) Chopped nuts (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until well blended.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the egg mixture until blended.
  5. If desired, fold in chopped nuts.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting into squares.

Additional Notes

  • You can add chocolate chips or chunks for extra chocolatey flavor.
  • For fudgier brownies, reduce the baking time slightly.
  • This recipe can be customized with various toppings like frosting, whipped cream, or ice cream.

Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups (200g) Graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup (50g) Granulated sugar
1/2 cup (115g) Butter, melted
24 oz (680g) Cream cheese, softened
1 cup (200g) Granulated sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
4 large Eggs
1 cup (240ml) Sour cream
1/4 cup (50g) All-purpose flour

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Blend in sour cream and flour until smooth.
  6. Pour the filling over the crust in the pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes or until the center is almost set.
  8. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven for 1 hour with the door slightly open.
  9. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

Additional Notes

  • You can top the cheesecake with fruit compote, fresh berries, or chocolate ganache before serving.
  • For a firmer texture, chill the cheesecake longer in the refrigerator.
  • Allow the cheesecake to come to room temperature before serving for the best flavor.

Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
12 Lasagna noodles
1 lb (450g) Ground beef or Italian sausage
1/2 cup (120ml) Chopped onion
2 cloves Garlic, minced
24 oz (680g) Tomato sauce
6 oz (170g) Tomato paste
1/2 cup (120ml) Water
2 cups (450g) Ricotta cheese
1/4 cup (30g) Grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp Dried basil leaves
1 lb (450g) Shredded mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef or sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water to the meat mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and dried basil.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  10. Let the lasagna stand for a few minutes before cutting into squares and serving.

Additional Notes

  • You can add layers of cooked spinach or mushrooms for a vegetarian option.
  • Allow the lasagna to cool slightly before serving to set and make it easier to slice.
  • This recipe can be prepared ahead and refrigerated before baking.

Crêpes Sucre Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cup (125g) All-purpose flour
2 large Eggs
1 1/4 cups (300ml) Milk
2 tablespoons Granulated sugar
2 tablespoons Butter, melted (plus more for cooking)
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract
Pinch of Salt

Preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients gradually, whisking until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  4. Heat a lightly buttered non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet and swirl it around to cover the bottom evenly.
  6. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes until the edges start to brown, then flip it and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking cooked crepes on a plate as you go.

Additional Notes

  • Serve the crêpes with various toppings such as Nutella, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or maple syrup.
  • You can adjust the sweetness by varying the amount of sugar in the batter.
  • These crêpes can be stored in the refrigerator and reheated before serving.
